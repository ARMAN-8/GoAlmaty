    Top 10 Diving Destinations in Türkiye

    When it comes to diving destinations, with its rich underwater world, Türkiye is a heaven for scuba divers! It offers many sea diving spots ranging from the Marmara Sea to the crystal-clear Aegean Sea and the warm and beautiful Mediterranean Sea. From east to west and north to south, the diversity of coral is stunning - not to mention the shipwrecks and the brilliant diversity of sea life, big and small! Türkiye’s underwater beauty will without a doubt leave a lasting impression on you! If you haven’t scuba dived before, there are plenty of diving centers where you can benefit from certified professional instructors. We’ve prepared a list of the top 10 destinations in Türkiye to scuba dive – they offer opportunities like deep sea diving, cave diving, and much more!

    Aquarium Cove, Fethiye

    If a relaxing scuba dive is what you’re seeking, Aquarium Cove, Fethiye offers a magnificent world underneath the surface. You’ll be charmed the moment you open your eyes underwater! The cove is famous for its diversity of fish and marine life. If you’re lucky, you might dive alongside octopuses and Mediterranean morays. Stunning scenery and epic expeditions await you!

    Big Reef, Bodrum

    The best way to discover the coral life of the Aegean Sea, is a dive at Big Reef, Bodrum! In the crystal-clear sea, you’ll get the chance to come close to a large coral reef. Starfish, sea urchins, and vibrant fishes will liven up your diving experience. We recommend arranging an early dive as this is the time the sea is at its clearest.

    To learn more about Bodrum, click here.

    Uçan Balık, Kaş

    Earning its name from the wreck of an Italian torpedo plane that sits deep beneath the surface, Uçan Balık (lit. Flying Fish) is one of the best destinations for experienced divers. Predator fishes such as dentexes, groupers, barracudas, and amberjacks are numerous in this area, and will delight you when the current is accommodating. Despite the challenges, a dive here is an adventure worth having!

    Ayvalık

    Much like the rest of Türkiye, Ayvalık is a great spot for scuba diving. The perfect underwater life of Ayvalık islands awaits to be discovered! Ayvalık is also suitable for those seeking to discover the world of scuba diving for the first time. You’ll never forget the moment when you’re met by a thousand different sea life species between the red corals. It’s very easy to take pictures too - don’t waste another moment to make unforgettable memories!

    Saros, Çanakkale

    In addition to being a reef dive, Saros offers a historical journey. The region’s historical background makes this an ideal destination for those looking for wreck diving. If you haven’t been here before, it is highly recommended. The underwater currents guarantee the cleanliness of the cove, offering divers a high-definition experience. Being generous with its underwater riches, Saros is highly favored by both amateur and experienced scuba divers.

    To discover what else you can do in Çanakkale, click on the link.

    Datça

    Datça is beautifully pristine and amazingly well preserved. It is a top destination for natural life, both on the surface and underwater. The water is so clean that you should trust your eyes if a a dolphin come up to greet you. The water temperature here is warm, and many would agree that Datça is one of the most biodiverse diving destinations in Türkiye. It is also perfect for great photos!

    Ready to discover this very special place? Check the link.

    Kuşadası

    Kuşadası is the secret treasure of the Aegean! It is a very special spot for underwater explorers. One of the best things about it is that there are no currents here. It contains beautiful underwater rock formations to admire as you dive deep. There are certified instructors here to help you in this deep journey. It’s also a good site for beginners because of the clarity and good visibility of the water – even in the shallower waters, it offers wonderful scenes of underwater life. You can also practice your underwater modeling moves as there are photographers ready to take unforgettable pictures for you!

    Follow the link to explore the other vibes of Kuşadası!

    Gökova

    Getting its name from “sky blue,” Gökova might truly be the bluest destination ever! Its beautiful waters have made it known to scuba divers the world over. The underwater world here contains groupers such as dusky groupers and dogtooth groupers, and if it’s autumn, you may even see tunas that weigh over 10 kilos. The harmony of the green, deep beneath the water, and the sunglint, will make you want to never leave Gökova!

    Marmaris

    Marmaris can satisfy people with all kinds of taste. With more than 50 diving spots, you’ll find astounding wrecks to explore, ancient ruins, and amazing reefs. It’s also suitable for every level of divers. A thrilling underwater world awaits you here! If you’re a beginner, you can dive under the close supervision of licensed instructors to help with your first steps into a world you’ve never seen before.

    To learn more about Marmaris, follow the link.

    Kalkan

    Enjoy diving in the sea in the calm waters of Kalkan in Kalamar Bay. Mediterranean rainbow wrasses, barracudas, sea breams, octopuses, and even sea turtles will compete for attention in the photos you take here! Kalkan is highly suitable for inexperienced divers as well. With its clear, sparkling Mediterranean waters, Kalkan will become your favorite sea diving destination – and for good reason!
